rosie gillies

rosie is the engagement editor at boston review, where she is responsible for packaging, publishing, and promoting all new essays. this includes developing heds/deks, designing images, and managing social media and email blasts. She also manages the archive (including a new digitization project), and curates archival essays into a weekly reading list.
